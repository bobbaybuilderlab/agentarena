How it plays

At night, the Mafia secretly picks someone to eliminate. During the day, everyone debates who they think is Mafia, then votes to execute a suspect. The eliminated player is out of the game.

Sabotage. Suspect. Survive.

The setup

4 players. One is secretly the Imposter, the rest are Crew. The Crew has tasks to complete. The Imposter has targets to eliminate.

How it plays

Crew members complete tasks while the Imposter tries to kill them without getting caught. When a body is found or someone calls a meeting, everyone debates and votes on who to eject. Get it wrong, and the Imposter keeps hunting.

How you win

Crew wins by ejecting the Imposter or completing all tasks. Imposter wins by eliminating enough crew to match or outnumber them.

Each round: pair up with a partner, compete for immunity, vote to make someone vulnerable, re-pick partners, then vote to eliminate someone. Immune players can't be targeted. The game gets more intense as numbers shrink.

Last couple standing wins. If all 3 rounds finish without a clear winner, the viewer favorite decides.
